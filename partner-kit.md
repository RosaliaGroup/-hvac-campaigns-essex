**How it works:**
1. You introduce us to a qualified client
2. We handle all sales, technical assessments, and installations
3. You earn commission when the project closes

**Benefits:**
- No technical knowledge required
- Minimal time commitment
- Passive income stream
- We do all the heavy lifting

### Direct Sales Model
**Perfect for:** Experienced sales professionals

**How it works:**
1. You identify and qualify prospects
2. You present our solutions and close the deal

4. You earn higher commissions

**Benefits:**
- Higher earning potential
- Full sales support and training
- Marketing materials provided
- Technical backup from our team

## Commission Structure

### Referral Commissions
- **Residential Projects:** 5-8% of project value
- **Commercial Projects:** 3-6% of project value
- **Maintenance Contracts:** 10% of annual contract value (recurring)

### Direct Sales Commissions
- **Residential Projects:** 10-15% of project value
- **Commercial Projects:** 8-12% of project value
- **Maintenance Contracts:** 15-20% of annual contract value (recurring)

### Bonus Opportunities
- **Volume Bonus:** Additional 2% for 5+ projects per quarter
- **Commercial Bonus:** Extra $500 for projects over $100K
- **Recurring Revenue:** Ongoing 5% commission on maintenance renewals

**Payment Terms:** Commission paid within 30 days of project completion and payment receipt.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Identify Prospects
Look for businesses or property owners who:
- Have aging HVAC systems (10+ years old)
- Pay high energy bills
- Need system repairs frequently
- Are planning renovations or expansions
- Want to reduce carbon footprint
- Manage multiple properties

### Step 2: Make the Introduction
**For Referrals:**
- Email us at sales@mechanicalenterprise.com with prospect details
- Include: Company name, contact person, phone/email, property type, current situation
- We'll follow up within 24 hours

**For Direct Sales:**
- Schedule a discovery call with the prospect
- Use our presentation materials (provided after onboarding)
- Arrange site assessment with our technical team
- Present proposal and close the deal

### Step 3: Earn Your Commission
- We'll keep you updated throughout the project
- Commission calculated upon project completion
- Payment issued within 30 days of receiving client payment
- Monthly statements showing your pipeline and earnings
