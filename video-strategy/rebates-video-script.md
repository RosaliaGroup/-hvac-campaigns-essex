## HEYGEN INTEGRATION PLAN

**Setup:**
1. Ana records a 15-second intro for each video (or uses a HeyGen AI avatar)
2. HeyGen renders the talking head clip
3. Clip is prepended to the AI-generated explainer in a simple video editor (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or HeyGen's built-in editor)
4. Final video uploaded to YouTube

**Personalization at Scale (Phase 2):**
- Use HeyGen's API to generate personalized video messages for each assessment lead
- "Hi [First Name], here is your personalized rebate breakdown for [Address]..."
- Triggered automatically when a lead completes the Rebate Calculator
